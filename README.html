Wheres the flood?

This repo offers a simple interface implementing the Nervous Norman prototype for the UFOKN project. The test cases intragetes NOAA National Water Model, Height above Nearest Drainage Data provided by the University of Texas, road infastructure from the US Census, and Building footprint information from Open Street Map.

The spatial scope of this product is the sity of Houston Texas and the temporal scope is the period of Hurricane Harvey (09/10/2017 - 08/18/2017).The archived NWM output for this event has been archived by CUAHSI here and is represenataive of the operational data that is avialbe in real-time for any location in the continential United States.
